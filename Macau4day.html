Day 1 Trip

Day 2 Trip

Nam Van Lake Sir Robert Ho Tung Library Sun Yat Sen Memorial House
You can go there at 10am. The center is a training ground for water sports athletes and an ideal venue for cultural and recreational activities, and carnivals. Plan to stay: 1-2 hours. It opens from 10am to 7pm,so you can go there at 2pm. The Sir Robert Ho Tung Library is a typical example of a beautiful mansion equipped with an arcade and a garden. Plan to stay: 1hour. It opens from 11am to 6pm, so you can go there at 4pm. It's impressive and well worth checking out, especially as there's no entrance fee. Plan to stay: 30 minutes.

Day 3 Trip

Rua du Cunha Macau Science Center Fisherman's Wharf
It opens from 10am to 9pm. You can bring Macau signature good back home to share with friends and you can find many on this street. Plan to stay: 2hours. It opens from 10am to 6pm. You can go there at 1pm. 3D movie theater is excellent.There are quite a few activities that can bring good attention. Plan to stay: 2-3 hours. Day 4 Trip

Lotus Square Grand Prix Museum Macau Wine Museum
The Lotus is a outdoor playland and adult playground. It opens from 12am to 3pm and from 6pm to 11pm.So we will go there at 12am. Plan to stay: 1-2 hours. The Grand Prix Museum, a motor racing museum, is inside Macau Tourism Activities Center. It opens from 10am to 6pm and closes on Tusdays. Plan to stay: 3hours. The Macau Wine Museum is a wine museum and it is co-located next to the Grand Prix Museum. It opens from 10am to 6pm and closes on Tusdays. Plan to stay: 1hour.
