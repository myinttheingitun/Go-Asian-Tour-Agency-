Singapore 4 days Trip

Day 1 Trip

Asian Civilizations Museum

This museum aims to present an integrated view of pan-Asian cultures. The museum highlights the civilizations which make up the nation's multi-ethnic community. From the Singapore gallery there is a large window with a view overlooking the river and city.

Merlion Park

Tourists come to the park to pose for a photo with the beloved Merlion.Take a break from Singapore to see Merlion Park. It's just a day trip away with Sentosa Island Afternoon Trip Including Merlion Tower and Tiger Sky Tower.



ArtScience Museum

At the base of the Marina Bay Sands Hotel is a structure resembling a lotus flower or the welcoming hand of Singapore.
Save valuable time and money by getting ArtScience Museum tickets in advance.





Day 2 Trip

Chinatown Singapore

The memories, traditions and history of Singapore's Chinese community are preserved here.Chinatown Heritage Centre is part of Uncover the Gems of Chinatown Singapore - a great way to experience Singapore's unique culture.

Esplanade Park

A number of monuments and memorials are positioned throughout the park.
Esplanade Park is part of Singapore River Cruise.
An enjoyable way to experience Singapore.

River Safari

The River Safari covers 12 hectares and is located between the Singapore Zoo and Night Safari.Singapore Super Saver: Night Safari and River Safari is one of the best choices to get into that special Singapore atmosphere.

Day 3 Trip

Sri Thendayuthapani Temple

The temple covers a large area and has a pleasant location.
This temple is also called Chettiars' Temple or Tank Road Temple
and together with the Sri Layan Sithi Vinayagar Temple is administered
by the Chettiars' Temple Society.

Clarke Quay

This quay is one of three quays on the Singapore River it lies upstream from the river mouth and Boat Quay. Clarke Quay by night is simply remarkable. Singapore Hop-On Hop-Off Night Tour is one of the best choices to get into that special Singapore atmosphere.

Singapore Art Museum

This museum was originally called the Fine Art Museum in 1996 when it opened as one of five museums in the Art and Heritage District.
Experience the taste of Singapore with Street Art & Food Tour - a culinary angle to Singapore Art Museum.

Day 4 Trip

Sentosa Island

This mega attraction is located on the island of Sentosa.
At Universal Studios Singapore there are amusement park rides and shows based on blockbuster movies and T.V. shows.
Marine Life Park includes several attractions and is the world's largest oceanarium.
At Dolphin Island learn about and interact with Bottlenose dolphins.
At Adventure Cove Waterpark you can have fun in a tropical paradise themed water park.
The Trick Eye Museum takes you into a world of 3D and uses a technique which turns 2D paintings into 3D images using optical illusions.

Marina Bay Sands

There are many reasons to visit the Marina Bay Sands.Marina Bay Sands after sunset is a unique experience. Singapore Night Tour: Gardens By the Bay, Marina Bay Sands SkyPark and River Cruise provides just that.
