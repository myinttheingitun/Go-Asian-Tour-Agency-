Singapore 3 days Trip

Day 1 Trip

Singapore International Airport Singapore Zoo Orchard Parade Hotel
We will pick you at Singapore international airport with our tour guide. We wil drive to Singapore Zoo.We can explore wildlife of animals there. Then, we will drive back to hotel and have a full rest in the evening.

Day 2 Trip

Marina Bay Sands Marina Bay Sands SkyPark Sands Sky Park Shopping Mall
This day, we will go to Marina Bay! We can relax there with fresh air,then take in the stunning views from the Sands SkyPark Observation Deck. It would not be complete if we do not visit SkyPark Observation Deck.We can surely get memorable time there. Offering wine and dine experience,it's sure to be an unforgettable meal.Be sure to take home some souvenirs from the Sands Sky Park Shop.

Day 3 Trip

Singapore Flyer River Safari Universal Studios Singapore
This day is for sightseeing. We will go to the Flyer. The breathtaking view aboard the Flyer promises an expansive view of the skyline, along with a brief glimpse of Malaysia and Indonesia from the top. Then,we will visit River Safari.The River Safari is a river-themed zoo and aquarium,designed to recreate the wildlife experience along famous rivers from around the world. Then, we will go to Universal Studios where old and young can have fun.After staying there for some hours, we will head to airport for homeward flight.
