Macau 3 days Trip

Day 1 Trip

Macau Airport St Dominic Church Macau Museum
Our tour starts from your arrival in Macau Airport and we will transfer to Yongchun Hotel. You can take a rest for a while, then you can start your trip. Plan to stay: 1-2 hours. It is a worth visiting place. You can go there from 10am to 6pm. The famous Ginger Milk shop YiShun is just next to the shop beside this church for breakfast. Plan to stay: 2hours. Small but comprehensive museum , gives a good overview of Macau's history. It opens from 10am to 5pm. Plan to stay: more than 2hours.

Day 2 Trip

Venetian Macau Kun Iam Ecumenical Galaxy Macau
Venetian Macau has many entertainment and interesting things. You can enjoy many shows like Monkey king show, So man show. Plan to stay: 4-5 hours. It opens from 10am to 6pm and closed on Friday. The centre will be open to public two hours after the Typhoon Warning Signal is lowered. Plan to stay: 1-2 hours. Galaxy Macau is a great Entertainment centre of Macau with hotels, resturants, and impressive things. You can stay Galaxy Macau Hotel for one night. Plan to stay: 12 hours.

Day 3 Trip

Camoes Garden and Grotto Macau Tower House of Dancing Water show
Camoes Garden at Camoes Square is one of the largest parks. It opens from 6am to 10pm. It is a good place for resting in a Chinese pavilion with stone tables and stools. Plan to stay: 1 hour. It opens at 6am to 9pm.So, you can get lunch at Macau Tower, a great place of 360 degree restaurant. You can try Skyjump there. Plan to stay: 1-2 hours. The House of Dancing Water feactures acrobatic elements and original acts, with a stage that transforms into different sets. Each show runs for 90 minutes. Plan to stay: More than 2 hours.
