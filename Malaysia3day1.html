Malaysia 3 days Trip

Day 1 Trip

Jalan Alor Merdeka Square Menara KL Tower
As lunchtime approaches, visit Jalan Alor in Bukit Bintang for a tantalising fill of Asian delicacies and street food. You can go there at 11am. Plan to stay: 2hours. You can swim in crystal clear waters, hike in verdant jungles, or even enjoy a picnic by stunning waterfalls. You can go there at 3pm. Plan to stay: 3hours. You can have a meal or enjoy high tea along with the most spectacular view of the city centre, bar chartering a helicopter. You can go there at 7pm. Plan to stay: 2hours.

Day 2 Trip

Batu Caves Genting Theme Park Colmar Tropicale
On your second day in Kuala Lumpur, visit Batu Caves so that you can admire the colourful Indian temples and architecture. You can go there at 8am. Plan to stay: 1-2 hours. Perfect for a night of unwinding amidst tranquil surroundings and breathtakingly verdant views, Genting Highlands. You can go there at 5pm. Plan to stay: More than 3hours. You can look to experience a classic French ambience amid lush tropical surroundings without splurging on a plane ticket. You can go there at 8pm. Plan to stay: 2hours.

Day 3 Trip

Pavilion Kuala Lumpur Central Market KL Chankat Bukit Bintang Nightlife
You can get reasonably priced Korean, Japanese and local fare and find the latest collections from international and local high fashion designers. You can go there at 10am. Plan to stay: 3hours. You can find plenty of handicraft and souvenir stalls, as well as boutiques selling authentic Malaysian batik prints and local artwork. You can go there at 2pm. Plan to stay: 3hours. You can enjoy two pints of Asahi for RM42 and two-for-one promos for all martinis.You can enjoy house wines like Australian Sauvignon Blanc. It opens from 5pm to 4am,so you can go at 8pm. Plan to stay: 4hours.
